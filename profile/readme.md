# Wunderkammer Labs

The *wunderkammer* was the cabinet of curiosities, a space where objects from nature, history, art, and science were gathered to provoke wonder and make sense of the world, and the institutional forerunner of the museum as we know it. Wunderkammer Labs builds open-source tools and platforms in that tradition.

We develop software, learning resources, and digital infrastructure for museums, archives, libraries, and cultural heritage organizations. Our work spans collection management, mobile interpretation, physical computing installations, augmented reality experiences, data workflows, and the documentation and training that help institutions actually put these tools to use.
